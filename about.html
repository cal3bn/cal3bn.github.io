About Me

I'm Caleb Neal, an IT student at Sinclair Community College. I plan on advancing to the University of Cincinnati for a degree in CyberSecurity/Cryptography. This website was a project to test my HTML5+CSS abilities. I've been surrounded and enamored by computers since before I can remember, and when I was as young as a third-grader teachers were calling me out of classes to troubleshoot basic computer issues. In High School I joined the USA CyberPatriot competition and gained experience in real-time threat analysis and prevention. In college I've joined the Sinclair Cyber Team and we've been gaining pentesting and technical writeup experience. In addition, I've played around with TryHackMe, DVWA (Insecure webapp), and some personal virtual machine setups to gain hands-on experience.
